About

Sierra George is a junior at The University of Colorado Boulder pursuing a degree in Advertising and minoring in Creative Technology and Design. She is currently the Assistant Marketing Manager for The Bold, a student run media publication at CU. She mainly does the graphic design work for the social medias and magazine. She has gained skills in photography, Adobe Creative Cloud, brand strategy, and leadership during her summer internship with Camp Wicosuta, where she was a media intern. After graduation she wishes to pursue a career in creative advertising to become an art director.
